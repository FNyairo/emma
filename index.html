About

Bridging Technology and Human Potential in East Africa

Dr. Emma Nkonoki is a Tanzanian-born, Finland-based researcher whose work sits at the intersection of information and communication technology, grassroots entrepreneurship, and inclusive development. Her doctoral dissertation — completed at the University of Turku in 2025 — examined how ICT can empower low-income entrepreneurs in East Africa's emerging innovation ecosystems.

With over 25 years in Finland and deep ties to Tanzanian academic circles (her late father was a professor of science and technology), Emma brings a uniquely cross-cultural lens to her research, shaped equally by Dar es Salaam and Helsinki.
