XR Tanzania - Extended Reality in Education

[PLACEHOLDER - Replace with the real project description when confirmed.]

This project investigates the feasibility and impact of Extended Reality (XR) technologies - encompassing Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, and Mixed Reality - in secondary and vocational education settings in Tanzania. Drawing on participatory design methods, the study aims to understand how XR can bridge resource gaps and provide immersive, equitable learning experiences for students who lack access to physical labs or textbooks. Initial fieldwork is planned for the Iringa and Dar es Salaam regions.

STEP-EA - Supporting Teachers in East Africa

[PLACEHOLDER - Replace with the real project description.]

STEP-EA is a capacity-building initiative focused on teacher well-being, digital pedagogical development, and professional support networks across Kenya, Tanzania, and Uganda. The project explores how peer-learning models and mobile platforms can be deployed to improve classroom outcomes in under-resourced schools.

PhD Dissertation - Grassroots ICT Innovation in East Africa

Article dissertation exploring how ICT fosters inclusive innovation among low-income entrepreneurs in Tanzania and broader East Africa. Combines qualitative fieldwork in innovation hubs, policy analysis, and case studies from Iringa, Silicon Dar, and Zanzibar.

Completed 📍 University of Turku 🗓 2019 - 2025
