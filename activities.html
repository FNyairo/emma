Presented research on inclusive innovation ecosystems at the United Nations University World Institute for Development Economics Research seminar series. Mentoring newly arrived immigrants on professional networking and workplace integration through Moniheli's "At Work in Finland" programme — a foreigner-to-foreigner model connecting experienced migrants with those navigating the Finnish job market.
