Consultancies & Teaching

Expertise for Hire &
Courses to Share

Emma works with NGOs, universities, and international development organisations as a strategic consultant - and is available to teach across ICT4D, research methodology, and innovation domains.

Consultancies

Areas of Consultancy Expertise

📊

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning

Designing and implementing M&E frameworks for development programmes across East Africa. Expertise in qualitative and quantitative data collection, impact assessment, and results-based management for NGOs and international development partners.

💡

Fundraising Strategy

Strategic guidance on fundraising for Finnish and East African NGOs, including grant writing, donor relations, and crafting compelling narratives that connect research evidence to development impact for European and Nordic funders.

🧒

Early Childhood Education Advisory

Advisory services for ECE curriculum design, teacher training programme development, and policy analysis in Sub-Saharan Africa. Drawing on multi-country fieldwork in Tanzania, Rwanda, and Kenya, with a focus on inclusive, community-centred approaches.

📱

ICT4D Strategy

Supporting organisations in designing technology-for-development initiatives that reach grassroots communities. Includes digital needs assessment, technology adoption barriers, and participatory design of mobile-based tools for low-resource settings.

🤝

NGO-Private Sector Collaboration

Facilitating and advising on partnerships between Finnish development NGOs and private sector actors in East Africa. Practical experience from the Fingo Kenya report and field-level partnership brokering across multiple sectors.

🌱

Innovation Ecosystem Development

Research-informed consultancy on building inclusive innovation ecosystems that incorporate grassroots actors, informal sector entrepreneurs, and women-led enterprises. From policy recommendations to on-the-ground hub development.

PG / UG
